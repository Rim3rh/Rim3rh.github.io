Our Last Hope

Our Last Hope, was the first game I developed, the whole development lasted 2 weeks, during which me and my partner worked on this project.

My Role On The Project

During the development of Hope, I worked as the main programmer, developing all the code, as well as the animations trees, menues, HUDs and all the interactions related to the projects code. Also, I did the animations for the main character, as well as some of the backgrounds, and level design.

Movement

For the movement of this game, we wanted a precise movement that would allow the player to parkour through the level. I added coyote jump and jump buffering so the player's mistakes wouldn't affect the player so much.

Attacks

For the attack, we made it so the player has to stay still while attacking. This attack consists on two separate attacks that when combined can kill the main enemies.The charged attack is obtained at the end of the level, and with it, you will have to defeat the final boss. When you use it, the character casts a powerful spell that makes the boos vulnerable, and once vulnerable, you can attack him to defeat him.

Dash

WThe Dash, is an extension of the playerMovement, with it, you can reach places you wouldn't be able to reach. As a second function, it deals damage to the enemies you go through when using it.

Boss Fight

The boss fight consists of a 3 stage fight, where you need to ignore the bosses attacks, and once dodged his attacks, you can attack him with your charged attack so he is vulnerable to your attacks.
