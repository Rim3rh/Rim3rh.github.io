Fate: The Cure Of Belzegor

Fate, is a 3d Side-Scroller, where you play as Fate, a young girl that will have to fight enemies to escape from the demon Belzegor.

My Role On The Project

During the development of Fate, I worked as the main programmer, developing all the code, as well as the animations trees, menus, HUDs and all the interactions related to the project's code.

Game Mechanics

Movement

With the movement we wanted to give the player as much control as possible on fate, so to archive that we opted for a acceleration based movement, using physics to enhance the feeling of jumps and hooks.

Attacks

For the attacks, we went with the classic 3 hit combo, having the first 2 basic attacks, and if you hit an enemy, you can attack again to unleash the third and strongest attack.

Parry

When balancing the parry, we were afraid that the lack of visual feedback, would make the parry too hard, so, to solve this, we gave it a bit more duration, and increased the difficulty on the basic enemy, that way, once you arrived at the boss, you would have good control on the parry.

Cameras

With the camaras, we wanted to use the third dimesion make a side-Scroller 3D gave us, so we made some of the areas have different camera angles, so you could really feel the 3D immersion.

Boss Fight

When designing the boss fight, we wanted to give the player a real chellange, we knew that by doing this we would make the more casual players unable to beat the boss, but we wanted Belzegor to be a chellange for the usual metroidvania players.
