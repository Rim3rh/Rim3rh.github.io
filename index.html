About Me

Hello! I'm Mario, I'm a game programmer focused on Unity C#. I have worked on multiple games including 2D side-scrollers, 2D fighters, Third person adventures, 3D side-scrollers, 3D First Person Shooters and VR Experiences. I am currently working on an Fps survival game as a personal project, and on a motion tracking game for TUAS GameLab developed with the ZED 2 AI camera. Right now, and what I enjoy the most is making 2D or side scroller games like Fate: The Curse Of Belzegor, or Our Last Hope, but I'm still trying new game genres and different game mechanics. Feel free to explore my portfolio to learn more about my work!

Time-Line

2020-2022

Vocational training course on Networking & computer sistems

After the obligatory education in spain, I decided to do the Vocational training course on Networking because i really liked computers, and wanted to learn alot about them, and thanks to this course, i did, additionaly, i got the basics on HTML, CSS and a small peack into bash. I learned about computer hardware, basic Networking basic civersecurity and advanced office usage. I loved the time spent there, so for the following years, I had a big question, follow the path i had started, continuing with either Networking, Web development or app development, or I could start studyng Game Development, a theme I had almost no idea about, and that from the outside, and with no previus experience, looked amazing.

2022-2023

Vocational training course on 3D Environments & Game Development

Once I took the decision of going into game development, I was able to try all the aspects of this sector, Drawing, Animating, Modeling, and Proggraming. From the begining, I knew Art was not made for me, althouhg i did enjoy it at times, I was never really good at it. Luckly, on my class, the ammount of programmers was around 1.5, wich made me stand out as the only person fully focusing on proggraming, and that made me work harder on my programming and learn even more.

Projects

Our Last Hope

This, as mentioned in the proyects section, was my first ever "finished" game. Developed in 2 weeks and since I dont want to bore you with the details(you have them on its page) i will just say I learned a ton from it.

(GJM)Square Root

This was my first ever Game Jam, and since all my experience was having done one game, I wasent able to take the lead on the proggraming of this game. On this game, I worked as an programmer assistant with some tasks like proggraming the menues, the basic character controller, and a small part of the games logic. That said, it was an amazing experience beeing able to work with really talented people and I learned a lot of things like what a SingleTone was(at 3Am). Unfurtunatly all I have of this project is its github.

Beyond Earth

This project, like most of the ones on my Vocational training course, had a duration of 2 weekks, and as a fun fact, sinse you can find all the information about it on its page, this proyect was we my girl friend and I started talking, wich probably made the project itself be wrose, but still, I learned a lot of things about 3D games in the 2 weeks this project lasted.

Voluntary work on RacconGameStudio

Once finished my first year on the vocational training course, and with all sumer ahead, i decided I would try to find an indi studio willing to let me work with them so i could improve my experience on game development. On an event one of my school teachers had organized, I went to the stand of RacoonGameStudio and asked the owner if he would allow me to work with them for summer. With the help of some of my teachers that tried to comvince him to "hire(voluntary work)" me, we ended up working together, and in that sumer, I learned a ton about game development, proggraming good practices and following the advise of the CEO, started to play games that would help me understand better how games worked and would help me on my game-knoledge.

VR scape Room

This project was developed in two weeks, as a test for my voluntary work on RacoonGameStudio. With no previus experience on any VR tecnologies, i learned a lot on how the device worked, the VR interactions and tons of other VR topics.

2023-2024

Vocational training course on 3D Environments & Game Development

Starting my second year on the course, I hade very clear ideas of what I wanted to do , wich in my case was proggraming, and thanks to the way this vocational training course is organized, working fully on my proggraming skills was possible. wich led me to work as the main proggramer of every project I worked on this year.
