Me La Pela

Me La Pela was a game born in the Glbal Game Jam, with the theme: "Make Me Laugh" for it, we wanted to make a 1v1 game where characters would insult there rivals when fighting, to add to the experience, we wanted to make character costumizable, that way you would have been able to play with a character showing your face and screming your fraces to his rival.

My Role On Me La Pela

During the 2 day Jam, I proggramed all the character select logic, as well as the menues, 2 player input management, and overall game flow(like winning rounds) and project management.

Character Selection

For the character selection, we worked with unity's new input system, and gave the player the possibility to choose between three characters.

Combat

For the combat, with limited time and experience with 1v1 games, we went for a simple 2D character controller with the ability to block incoming attacks, we wanted the focus of the game to have fun watching your own characters fight each other, so we didn't want to make combat feel complex to learn.
