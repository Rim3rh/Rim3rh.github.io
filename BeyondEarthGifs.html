Beyond Earth

BeyondEarth is a relaxing adventure game developed in 2 weeks. In it you play as an with the mission of surviving long enough to fix his ship and return to Earth.

My Role On The Project

During the development of Beyond Earth, I worked as the main programmer, developing all the code, as well as the animations trees, menues, HUDs and all the interactions related to the projects code.

Game Mechanics

Object Grabbing

When thinking about the game, we wanted to make a core pick-up mechanic, and with it, you can pick almost any item, and take it to a different position, giving you the opportunity to repair the ship and to stay alive.

Changing Oxigen

To survive, you will have to grab the empty oxygen tank, take it to the oxygen refill machine, and wait for it to be refilled. once refilled, you will be able to change it for your current one.

Rebuilding Ship

To uncover the parts lost from the space ship, you will have to grab the shovel and dig the piles of lunar dust.

Digging

Once undigged the parts, you will be able to grab them, take them to the rocketship, and repair them. When fully repaired, you will be able to return to earth.
