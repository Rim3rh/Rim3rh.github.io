Ayaan

Ayaan, was a small project I worked on as a class assignment. In it, you play as Ayaan, a girl that has the power to control stone, and with it, you will have to catch the 3 stones that scaped. During the level, you will have to use Ayaan's powers to pass the different obstacles.

My Role On The Project

During the development of Ayaan, I worked as the main programmer, developing all the code, as well as the animations trees, menues, HUDs and all the interactions related to the projects code.

Movement

When planning the movement, we wanted to have a fast paced game, in which the player would feel like a surfer going down a wave, our biggest encounter when trying to do that was the colliders that the map had. Issue that is yet to be resolved and that I would love to resolve once we have the time to get back on this project.

Jump

The jump is a simple mechanic that allows the player to jump over obstacles, hills and cliffs. Once on the air, if you maintain the jump button pushed, you will start to rotate. If you manage to complete a full rotation, you will be rewarded with so game points, air time will also be rewarded.

Boost

The boost allows the player to reach distances that he would otherwise not reach, obtain coins and obtain stones. To get it, you will have to click on the "boost" icon once it turns white, that will indicate it is ready to be used.

Rebuild Ability

Rebuilding will allow the player to go over areas that it is impossible to go through just with jumps or boosts. When rebuilding, you will have to push the "rebuild" icon, like mentioned before with the boost. The difference will be, once you press it, Ayaan will cast the spell, and the bridge will start to build, which means, if you are not fast enough, Ayaan will not be able to build the bridge in time
